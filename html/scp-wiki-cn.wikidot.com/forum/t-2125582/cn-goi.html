還沒看到下面，主要提ing問題
– hide block

they usually have strong territoriality, and communicating in Chinese. For these reason, they are assigned and handling by Foundation's Chinese branch.

前面那個應與have對稱用原型，後面那個變成他們處理，他們變成處理的人而不是被處理的。

Galactic Federation is a communicability organization that combining with religion and science fiction.

這是一個形容詞子句，主詞是that沒有be動詞，應該用原型或第三人稱單數型，(organization我一直當it處理，只是這種集合名詞的單複數我自己也不是很肯定)

另外是，沒記錯用上with表示主詞與受詞有相同性質或相同地位

able to communicating in hive-mind, and __creating __anomalous

able to 的to 是不定詞的to，應接原型

unable to ( ) against the Foundation operations or local government's enforcements, they also unable to use their anomalous ability effectively, but sometime their artifact are losing control and cause incidents.

不確定這是不是正確的，因為against不是個動詞，只是介系詞

Their philosophies are combined with Buddhism and space science fiction, these combination seems absurd and exhibited a creed that looks "ridiculous". But following with these philosophies and creed, they are still producing new anomalous items.

不懂怎麼突然過去式了，前面的對稱動詞是第三人稱單數啊

It cause bad influence to Foundation's operations since it hasn't weakening their threaten, but increasing difficulty that Foundation agents reach their core hierarchy.

it當主詞動詞加s，has後面加過去分詞不是ing，另外畢竟是書面還是避免縮寫比較好，threaten是動詞不是名詞。
